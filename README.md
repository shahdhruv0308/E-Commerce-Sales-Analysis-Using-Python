# E-Commerce-Sales-Analysis-Using-Python
This project analyzes a retail e-commerce dataset to uncover sales trends, profitability patterns, customer behavior, and product performance. Using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, various business questions were answered through exploratory data analysis and visualization
E-Commerce Sales Analysis | Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

Analyzed 9,994+ retail transactions to identify sales and profitability trends.
Built visualizations for monthly sales, category performance, customer segments, and profit analysis.
Evaluated sales-to-profit ratios and discount impact on business profitability.
Generated actionable business insights to support inventory, pricing, and marketing decisions.
Performed end-to-end EDA including data cleaning, KPI tracking, and dashboard-ready reporting.
